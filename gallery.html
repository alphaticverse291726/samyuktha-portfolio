How to add photos to your gallery

  1. Save your photo files into the assets/gallery/ folder (JPG or PNG recommended)
  2. Open gallery.html and find the gallery grid section
  3. Copy a .gallery-item block from the TEMPLATE comments at the bottom
  4. Change the src to assets/gallery/your-photo.jpg
  5. Update the data-cat attribute: professional, events, personal, or work
  6. Fill in the caption text and sub-caption, then push to GitHub
